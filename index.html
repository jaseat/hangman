How to play

Click new game to start new game

Press keys to make guesses (only alphabetic keys are accepted)

Each stage you start with 10 health

Every incorrect guess you lose health

Depending on the stage you may lose more health per guess

There are six stages in total

After finishing all six the game loops back to the beginning
